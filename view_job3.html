    Requirements

    Skills

Job Description

As a Remote Backend Engineer at Capgemini, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining backend systems and APIs to support various applications and services. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements, architect solutions, and deliver high-quality code that meets performance, scalability, and reliability standards.

You will work closely with frontend developers, product managers, and other stakeholders to ensure seamless integration of frontend and backend components. Additionally, you will participate in code reviews, troubleshooting, and performance optimization activities to continuously improve our systems and processes.
